Karere ka Ngoma

Akarere ka Ngoma ni kamwe mu majyepfo y'u Rwanda, kagaragaramo ikirenge cya Buganza cya Rutsiro. Karere ni kirazwi cyane kubera ubucuti bw'ibihugu bike n'imitako.

Ikagaba gikuru cyifashishwa mu gutwara abantu n'ibicuruzwa bikurikiranwa mu gihugu no mu mahanga. Akarere kagizwe n'imitenge 14.

Akarere ka Ngoma kazihugu gahutu, gahutu, ubukungu bw'akarere bushishikajwe cyane mu mirire y'ibikoni, ubucuruzi, n'ubuhinzi.

UBURYO BWO GUSABA UBWENEGIHUGU BW'U RWANDA

Ubwenegihugu bw'u Rwanda ni uburenganzira butuma umuntu amenya ko ari Umunyarwanda byemewe n'amategeko. Ubu burenganzira bumwemerera kugira indangamuntu y'u Rwanda, pasiporo y'igihugu, no kwitabira serivisi za Leta zose zigenwe ku baturage b'igihugu.

Ubwenegihugu bushobora kuboneka mu buryo butandukanye, bitewe n'impamvu umuntu asaba kuba Umunyarwanda:

I. UBWENEGIHUGU BUBONEKA KUBERA KUVUKA

Umuntu aba Umunyarwanda igihe:

  • Avutse ku babyeyi bombi b'Abanyarwanda, cyangwa
  • Avutse kuri umwe mu babyeyi ari Umunyarwanda

Inyandiko zisabwa:

  • Icyemezo cy'amavuko (Acte de naissance)
  • Kopi y'indangamuntu z'ababyeyi cyangwa icyemezo cy'ubwenegihugu bwabo
  • Icyemezo cy'aho utuye gitanzwe n'inzego z'ibanze
  • Amafoto 2 ya passport
  • Ifishi yo gusaba ubwenegihugu yuzuye

II. UBWENEGIHUGU BUBONEKA KUBERA GUSHYINGIRWA

Umunyamahanga washyingiranwe n'Umunyarwanda ashobora gusaba ubwenegihugu igihe amaze kubana n'uwo mwashyingiranywe igihe cyemewe n'amategeko.

Inyandiko zisabwa:

  • Icyemezo cy'ishyingirwa cyemewe n'amategeko
  • Kopi y'indangamuntu cyangwa pasiporo y'uwo mwashyingiranywe
  • Icyemezo cy'uko mubana mu buryo bwemewe (cohabitation certificate)
  • Icyemezo cy'imico myiza (Police clearance)
  • Amafoto 2 ya passport
  • Ifishi yo gusaba ubwenegihugu

III. UBWENEGIHUGU BUBONEKA KUBERA GUTURA MU RWANDA IGIHE KIREKIRE (NATURALIZATION)

Umunyamahanga umaze gutura mu Rwanda byemewe n'amategeko igihe kirekire (akenshi nibura imyaka 10) ashobora gusaba ubwenegihugu.

Inyandiko zisabwa:

  • Icyemezo kigaragaza igihe umaze utuye mu Rwanda
  • Kopi ya pasiporo n'impushya zo gutura (Residence permit)
  • Icyemezo cy'akazi, ubucuruzi cyangwa indi mirimo yemewe
  • Icyemezo cy'imisoro (Tax clearance)
  • Police clearance yo mu Rwanda n'iyo mu gihugu wavukiyemo
  • Amafoto 2 ya passport
  • Ifishi yo gusaba ubwenegihugu
  • Ibaruwa isobanura impamvu usaba kuba Umunyarwanda

IV. UBWENEGIHUGU BUBONEKA KUBERA IMPAMVU ZIDASANZWE

Hari abantu bahabwa ubwenegihugu kubera impamvu zidasanzwe zirimo:

  • Gutanga umusanzu ukomeye mu iterambere ry'u Rwanda
  • Impamvu z'ubumuntu cyangwa iz'igihugu

Inyandiko zisabwa:

  • Ibaruwa isobanura impamvu zidasanzwe ushingiraho
  • Ibyemezo bigaragaza umusanzu wawe ku Rwanda
  • Icyemezo cy'imico myiza
  • Amafoto 2 ya passport
  • Ifishi yo gusaba ubwenegihugu

V. AHO USHIKIRIZA DOSIYE

Dosiye yose ishyikirizwa ku:

Directorate General of Immigration and Emigration (DGIE)

Ni ho dosiye isuzumirwa, hakarebwa niba inyandiko zuzuye kandi ziri mu buryo bwemewe n'amategeko, hanyuma hafatwe icyemezo cya nyuma cyo kwemeza cyangwa kwanga ubwenegihugu.

VI. ICYITONDERWA CY'INGENZI

  • Inyandiko zose zigomba kuba ari umwimerere (originaux) cyangwa kopi zemejwe n'inzego zibifitiye ububasha
  • Inyandiko zo hanze y'u Rwanda zigomba kuba zahinduwe mu Kinyarwanda cyangwa mu Cyongereza, zemejwe n'inzego zibifitiye ububasha
  • Gutanga amakuru atari yo bishobora gutuma dosiye yangwa
  • Kuzuza neza ifishi yo gusaba ubwenegihugu ni ingenzi cyane
